# Franklin is a **Mac mesh cell** on the **shared vQbit** plane

> **Reconciliation (load-bearing):** On the **Mac development boundary**, **Franklin** is the **obligatory admin cell** on every host: GAMP5, zero-human drivers, `admin-cell`, father wallet, and inventory. **Each domain** that runs on that Mac is also a **cell**. Franklin and those domain cells **share the same vQbit substrate** (same `vQbitPrimitive` ABI, same observe/measure/entangle semantics, same evidence discipline via `fo_cell_substrate` and the plan’s vQbit surface) — not two disconnected universes. Franklin is not “outside” the mesh; it is the **local administrative cell** in the same manifold as the workloads it qualifies and heals.

1. The **sovereign network** mesh (nine-cell Klein bottle, quorum, per-IQ cell identities) remains defined in [mesh-topology.md](./mesh-topology.md) and [cell-taxonomy.md](./cell-taxonomy.md). **Mother-side** and **web/Docker** services are **out of the current Mac-cell track**; this doc is about the **on-Mac** stack only.
2. **Every Mac** that participates in the mesh story is expected to run **one Franklin** alongside the **domain** cells deployed there. Growth and healing apply to the **whole** local stack.

**Domain-agnostic (not fusion-specific):** the same **admin cell** model applies to **FSD, Drone Swarm, ATC, Maritime, Biomedical, Municipal**, and any other cross-domain use of the Mac that shares the same constitutional discipline. Franklin is the **on-device** discipline layer; domain rows stay in their own product tables, but the **vQbit story is unified** with Franklin, not siloed.

**Receipt families:** `franklin_mac_admin_gamp5_receipt_v1` is a **Mac Admin** run receipt — **not** a substitute for cell IQ/OQ/PQ **requirement** IDs. Keep requirement IDs in their own traceability matrix.

That does **not** mean Franklin is “not a cell” — it is the **local admin cell**; Mother coordinates across the **network** mesh.
