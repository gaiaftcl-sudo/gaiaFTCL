# Cell taxonomy — two nines (do not conflate)

**[I]** *Father* / *Mother* appear below as **analogies** (see [franklin-role.md](./franklin-role.md)).

**First use — two different “nines”:** (1) **Nine sovereign mesh cells** (Klein bottle, 5-of-9 quorum, geographic split) ≠ (2) **Nine canonical fusion plant *kinds*** (Tokamak, Stellarator, … — **physics substrates** GaiaFusion measures). Do not merge these in prose.

## Nine mesh cells

The **sovereign nine-cell mesh** (quorum, Klein bottle, NATS, wallet IDs per IQ) is the **operational** topology. Father/Mother/domain language applies to **this** “nine,” not to plant kinds. See [mesh-topology.md](./mesh-topology.md).

## Nine fusion plant *kinds*

The **nine canonical fusion plant kinds** (Tokamak, Stellarator, …) are a **physics/plant** catalog. They are **not** the same set as the nine **mesh** nodes.

**Rule:** a Fusion- or program-facing page must say explicitly whether it means **mesh cells** or **plant kinds** when it uses the word “nine.”

## Father, Mother, domain **[I]**

- **[I]** *Father* / *Mother* are **analogies** for how **local** substrate and **distributed** coordination are discussed — **inferred** language for human operators, distinct from a **sovereign network** cell ID in the nine slot inventory.
- **Father (Franklin):** one per Mac, the **local admin mesh cell**; GAMP / zero-human / `admin-cell` plane. Domain workloads on the machine are **domain cells**; all share the **vQbit substrate** on that Mac. Franklin is not an extra C-00n in the **network** nine — it is a **per-Mac** cell role. See [franklin-role.md](./franklin-role.md).
- **Mother:** peer/threshold **coordinator** pattern under **C-004** — draft semantics until mesh owner sign-off ([`MOTHER_DRAFT.md`](../../cells/franklin/MOTHER_DRAFT.md)); **never** a single mandatory central server.
- **Domain:** cross-domain products (FSD, swarm, ATC, …) re-use the **same** constitutional machinery; they are **not** new mesh-inventory slots by themselves.
