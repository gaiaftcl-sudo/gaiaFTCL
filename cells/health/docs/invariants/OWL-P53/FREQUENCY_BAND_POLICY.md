# Frequency band policy — OWL-P53 package (v1)

**Chosen option:** **B — Shipping path** (per reviewed plan)

## Policy

- **`INVARIANT_SPEC.md`** does **not** fix five numeric Hz bands inline in v1. It states only that the **frequency channel** exists, carries **FFT / band-power / phase** derived features, and is **epistemically tagged** `(T)` Tested at minimum until PQ evidence supports promotion.
- **Candidate band lists** (including literature-cited **444, 540, 372, 630, 348 Hz** windows where they appear in unified-frequency / UI draft docs) live only in **[`FREQUENCY_ADDENDUM.md`](FREQUENCY_ADDENDUM.md)**, each labeled **`(T)` Tested** or **`(A)` Assumed** with **pre-registration** intent and explicit **PQ** correlation criteria to the **other four channels**.

## Why

Avoids presenting unsettled band numbers as **normative** in the core invariant spec before cohort evidence exists, and reduces confusion with non-clinical “healing frequency” narratives in external literature.

## Relation to Option A

If future CCR moves to **Option A (rigorous)**, promote bands from **candidate** to **empirically derived** only when pre-registered PQ analysis meets agreed correlation thresholds; update **`FREQUENCY_ADDENDUM.md`** and epistemic tags `(T)`→`(M)` per channel.
