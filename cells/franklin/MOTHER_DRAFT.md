## Intent (draft)

**Mother** is the **[I]** *analogy* for **distributed coordination** / **threshold** patterns (watchers, rendezvous, consensus helpers) that still respect **no single point of constitutional truth** outside the signed receipt + substrate rules.

## C-004 alignment

- **Centralized** Mother (single mandatory server for constitutional control-plane traffic) is **out of scope** from day one. Prefer **P2P** + **replicated-with-threshold** / **gossip** / **cell-local** decisioning; threshold signatures on decisions are **aligned** with C-004 and narrow the design space *before* an owner is named.
- A **hosted** service may exist for **non-constitutional** convenience (artifacts, mirrors) only if labeled **non-authoritative** and **out of band** from C-001 receipts.
